About Us

Vibe Cartons is the perfect partner for your restaurant business!

We are a North Country distributor specializing in import and distribution of restaurant supplies! Established in 2020 and located in Poway, CA, we can support your supply needs wherever you are located around the San Diego area. We have our own fleet of delivery trucks that service all of our customers and can dispatch with minimal notice.
